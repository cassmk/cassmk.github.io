Oaken Preserves

For this project we were given a company to create a design for. With the given text and elements (ingredients, barcodes) we created 4 designs for 4 different flavours. Using Illustrator and InDesign I created these boxes and labels. First, I made the dieline for the box and label based on the product dimensions. I then drew the fruits in Illustrator, and placed the text and images in InDesign. The “Oaken Preserves” logo was provided.
